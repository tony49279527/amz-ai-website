## II. Competitor Analysis & Strategy

### 2.1 Our Product Analysis (B01BKT63XM)

**Strengths:**
*   **Beginner Safety:** 8mm throw DA is very forgiving.
*   **Ecosystem:** The "Brand Power" of Chemical Guys is huge. Users love the "Lifestyle".
*   **Kit Value:** Getting pads and liquids in the box is a massive plus.
*   **Support:** Thousands of YouTube tutorials.

**Weaknesses:**
*   **Power:** 700W is decent, but struggles with hard clear coats (vs 1000W rivals).
*   **Ergonomics:** Lack of a D-handle or Side-handle makes long jobs tiring.
*   **Pad Durability:** Basic pads wear out fast.

### 2.2 Core Competitor Analysis

| Competitor | Type | Pros | Cons |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Milwaukee M18** | Pro Cordless | Beast mode. Cordless freedom. | Expensive ($250+ tool only). Rotary can burn paint. |
| **Griot's Garage G9** | Enthusiast DA | 1000W Power, Lifetime Warranty, Detachable Cord. | More expensive ($170), no kit included usually. |
| **WEN 10PMC** | Budget Waxer | Super cheap ($40). | **Not a Polisher**. No cutting power. Just spreads wax. |
| **SPTA / Maxshine** | Chinese Value | Cheap ($60-80), Mini sizes available. | QC Lottery, poor instructions, vibration. |

### 2.3 Competitive Strategy

**Key Insight:** We sell "Confidence," not just a motor. We are the "Gateway Drug" to detailing.

**Operational Advice:**
1.  **"Starter Kit" Positioning:** Own the "My First Polisher" market.
2.  **Education First:** "Scan for 3-Step Guide" on the box.
3.  **Upgrade Path:** Since pads wear out, push "Hex-Logic Quantum" pad upgrades as upsells.
4.  **Handle Mod:** Sell a "Strap-on Handle" accessory or include one.
