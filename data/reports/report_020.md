### 1.2 Core Use Cases & Scenarios

The utility cart category serves a spectrum of use cases, ranging from simple transport to complex, integrated workstations. These scenarios can be prioritized from the most common to the most specialized, based on the frequency and detail in customer reviews.

This is the foundational use for all carts in this category. It involves moving a variety of loose or contained items from one location to another within a home, garage, or small commercial space. This use case demands a **stable, easy-to-roll platform with adequate shelf depth to prevent items from falling off**. The 2.4‚Äù deep tub shelves on B09XB5Z5QT are a direct response to this need. **Intermediate Use Case: Mobile Workstation**

**Specific Use Case: Heavy-Duty Industrial Hauling**  
At the top end of the spectrum is the use of the cart for moving very heavy, dense, or bulky items in a demanding environment. **Niche Use Case: Mobility Aid & Daily Living Assistant**
